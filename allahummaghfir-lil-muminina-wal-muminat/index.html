Allahummaghfir Lil Muminina Wal Muminat

Written by Heru Jun 16, 2021 · 10 min read

Allahummaghfir Lil Muminina Wal Muminat. The dua is, ‘allahummaghfir lil muslimina wal muslimat, wal mu’minina wal mu’minat’ (ya allah please forgive all muslim men and women, believing men and women). Allahummaghfir lil muslimina wal muslimat, wal mu’minina mal mu’minat, al ahyaai minum wal amwat, innaka ‘ala kulli syai’in qodir. Allahummaghfir lil muslimina wal muslimat, wal mu’minina mal mu’minat, al ahyaai minum wal amwat, innaka ‘ala kulli syai’in qadir. Robbanaghfirlana wa liikhwaaninal ladzina sabaquuna bil iiman wa laa taj’al fii quluubina ghilla lilladziina aamanu robbana innaka roufur rokhiim.
